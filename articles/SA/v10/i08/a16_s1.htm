Installation of Fwreport

Installation of fwreport is a very simple task. Simply follow these steps:

1. g++ fwreport.cpp (Listing 1)(or c++, or whatever your c++ compiler is).

2. Rename a.out (or the name your compiler outputs) to fwreport.

3. Open mail.pl (Listing 2); replace "youraddress\@yourdomain.com" with the appropriate values. Make sure to leave the "\" in before the "@" symbol; otherwise the script will die without sending you your message.

4. That's it -- you're done!
