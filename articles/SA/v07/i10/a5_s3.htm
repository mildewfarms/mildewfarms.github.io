Sidebar : Related Reading

The following Web sites are loaded with good information on timing products and other time-related information.

The ntp home page, maintained by the author of ntp, David Mills. This site contains the ntp distribution and links to other time-related sites:

http://www.eecis.udel.edu/~ntp/

The National Institute of Standards and Technology home page. A good site for time information, UTC and related time (ACTS) distribution software:

http://www.bldrdoc.gov/timefreq/

Time and date notation:

http://www.ft.uni-erlangen.de/~mskuhn/iso-time.html

Datum Inc.'s (and Bancomm) home page, a manufacturer of time processing equipment:

http://www.datum.com/

TrueTime's home page, a manufacturer of time processing equipment:

http://www.truetime.com/

Horology is the science of time, timekeepers (clocks, watches), and timekeeping:

http://www.horology.com/
