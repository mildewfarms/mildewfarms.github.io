Figure 2: -t flag output

The -t flag displays the three times stored in the
stat record.

stat -t /unix
access time: Mon Jan 31 08:32:53 1994
mod time: Wed Aug 18 09:31:46 1993
change time: Wed Aug 18 09:32:15 1993
