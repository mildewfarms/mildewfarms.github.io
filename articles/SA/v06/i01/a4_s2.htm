Sidebar 2: How do you know when you are doing a good job?

  • Disaster Prevention Checklist is complete.

  • All systems are fixed proactively and do not crash.

  • The system administrators are never called at home.

  • The computer system users are relatively happy.

  • Everything seems to work correctly.

  • The system administrators have time to be cordial.
