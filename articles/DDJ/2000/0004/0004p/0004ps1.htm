Name That Programming Language

The language JJ needs a new name. Consequently, we are having a "Name That Programming Language" contest and would like your help. Take a look at the JJ environment at http:// jj.caltech.edu/, then go to http://www .publicstaticvoidmain.com/NameJJ.html or you can go to http://www.ddj.com/jj/NameThatLanguage.html and make your suggestion. Suggesting the winning name will result in a glad bag of goodies for you, including DDJ CDs, books, an official DDJ T-shirt and hat, and even a bag of Dr. Dobb's Java coffee, which is "good to the last byte." We look forward to seeing your suggestions.

-- J.K., J.M., and D.E.
