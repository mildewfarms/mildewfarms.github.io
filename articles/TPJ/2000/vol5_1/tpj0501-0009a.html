The USENET Perl Newsgroups
The Perl Journal, Spring 2000
 

• comp.lang.perl.misc: General discussion about Perl.
• comp.lang.perl.moderated: Like c.l.p.misc, but higher quality.
• comp.lang.perl.tk: Discussion of the Perl/Tk graphical toolkit.
• comp.lang.perl.modules: Discussion of particular Perl modules.
• alt.perl: Perl discussion for those who don’t know about c.l.p.misc.
• comp.lang.perl.announce: Certain messages selected by the newsgroup owners.
