TPJ One-Liner #53

This little one-liner flattens an arbitrary "list of lists" (of letters or numbers for instance):

perl -MData::Dumper -le '@foo = ("abc",[1,[2]],[3,['d',['e',['f']]],4],5,[0.667,8,9],0.0); \

$bar = Dumper(\@foo); $bar =~ s/[\s\[\]=;]|\$VAR1//g; \

print join ", ", split /,/, $bar'

'abc', 1, 2, 3, 'd', 'e', 'f', 4, 5, '0.667', 8, 9, '0'
